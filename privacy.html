Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters

  • Journal entries are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store them.
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) is handled by iOS; we never access your biometric data.
  • Reminder notifications are scheduled locally on your device.
  • If you use Chat, requests are sent to OpenAI to generate responses. We do not retain your chat content on our servers. See OpenAI's terms for their processing.
  • We do not sell personal data and do not use analytics trackers.
  • You can export or delete your entries at any time from within the app.
